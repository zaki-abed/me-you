About Us

Me & You Driving School offers both intensive and semi-intensive driving lessons in Milton Keynes and the surrounding areas with a Government Approved Driving Instructor (ADI).

I offer a range of driving courses and driving lesson packages to suit your individual needs at highly competitive prices. Alternatively, you can also book driving lessons in Milton Keynes on a ‘pay as you go’ basis, especially if you only need a few lessons before your driving test.
